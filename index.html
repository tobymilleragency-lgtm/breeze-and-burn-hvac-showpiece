The Method

The Scientific Commissioning Loop

01

Expose & Scan

We mount specialized manometer probes and thermal cameras to map energy leakage throughout the home's shell.

02

Isolate Faults

We cross-reference sensor diagnostics to identify specific thermal flow drops, leaky ducts, or restricted filters.

03

Calibrated Repair

We clean, repair, and adjust your mechanical systems, tracking the restoration against precise static curve targets.

04

Verify & Seal

We run a secondary flow scan post-repair, handing over a printed audit validating the restored system efficiency.
